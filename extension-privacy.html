Shiloh Schedule: Extension — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

Overview

Shiloh Schedule: Extension is designed to provide a simple class period countdown and optional visual customization for users of the Shiloh Schedule website. We respect your privacy and minimize all data collection.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Only

The extension stores the following on your device, using Chrome’s built-in storage.sync system:

  • Class schedule selections
  • Visual theme or gradient preferences

This information is stored locally, and never transmitted to any server.

Site Integration

The extension may read schedule or theme information displayed on the Shiloh Schedule website to provide its functionality.
No personal information is collected, stored, or shared.

How We Use Information

  • Data is used exclusively to operate the extension (class countdown, theme display).
  • No information is sold, shared, or transmitted externally.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind is used.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not use external APIs, third-party code, or analytics services.
It only interacts with the Shiloh Schedule website.

Contact

Policy Changes
